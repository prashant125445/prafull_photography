The Studio

Storytelling, frame by frame

We're a small wedding photography studio built around one idea — your day should feel exactly like it happened when you look back at it years later. No forced poses, no rush. Just honest frames, careful editing, and albums made to last generations.

From the first "haldi" morning to the last dance at the reception, we cover it all: traditional photography, candid coverage, cinematic video, drone shots, and short-form reels for your socials.

Services & Pricing

Every wedding is different, so packages can be combined and customised. Here's how each service is priced.

01 — Photography

Camera Booking

₹3,000 / day

Traditional photography coverage, billed per day of camera booking.

Includes all RAW photos, delivered to you both on pendrive and directly to your phone. No printed album included.
02 — Album

Photo Album

₹400 / sheet

Priced per album sheet — each sheet holds 8 photos.

Example: 400 photos selected → 50 sheets → 50 × ₹400 = ₹20,000.
Cover: Normal ₹1,500 · Attached box cover ₹3,000.
03 — Video

Traditional Video

₹3,500 / hr

Classic wedding videography, billed per hour of coverage.

Most weddings run up to 3 hours of footage → 3 × ₹3,500 = ₹10,500.
04 — Aerial

Drone Coverage

₹4,000 / day

Aerial shots and sweeping venue visuals for your wedding film.

Booked per day alongside your main coverage.
05 — Candid

Candid Photography

₹4,000 / day

An additional camera dedicated purely to candid, unposed moments.

Runs alongside traditional coverage as a separate camera and photographer.
06 — Reels

Instagram Reels

₹1,500 starting

Short-form highlight reels edited for Instagram, ready to share.

Shot on iPhone — ₹1,500 · Shot on camera — ₹2,000.
All prices in INR and can be combined into custom wedding packages. Final quote depends on event duration, locations and deliverables — get in touch for a exact estimate.
